We Will

We will is an EP (extended play) by the band Kessoku Band. It was announced on September 1, 2024 alongside other informations of Kessoku Band ZEPP TOUR 2024 "We Will", and the name is the same with the name of the tour series. The EP will be released in digital and streaming platforms on September 6, 2024 and physical release follows on November 6, 2024.
